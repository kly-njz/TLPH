Permission Levels

  • View: Can see entries & reports
  • Create/Edit: Prepare journal entries
  • Submit: Send for approval
  • Approve: Post to ledger
  • Reverse: Undo posted entries
  • Export: Download reports

Entity Scopes

  • Own Municipality: Single municipality
  • Own Region: Region + all its municipalities
  • National Level: All regions & municipalities
  • All Entities: Read-only across all (Auditor)
