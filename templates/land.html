Land and Mineral Management

Land and Mineral Management programs oversee the proper use and distribution of land and mineral resources. These programs ensure that land development and mining activities are conducted responsibly and in accordance with environmental laws.

Mining operations are regulated to minimize environmental damage, including soil erosion, water contamination, and habitat destruction. Rehabilitation of mined areas is also required to restore ecosystems after resource extraction.

Additionally, land management includes land classification and titling to support economic growth and social equity. By balancing development and conservation, these programs promote sustainable land use for future generations.
